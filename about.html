ABOUT

Club History & Establishment

The Robotics Club of NIT Patna was established with the aim of creating a strong culture of hands-on engineering and innovation among students. What started as a small group of enthusiastic learners has grown into an active technical community working on diverse domains such as robotics, automation, and intelligent systems. Over the years, the club has encouraged students to move beyond theoretical learning by engaging in projects, workshops, and competitions. Today, the club serves as a platform where beginners learn fundamentals and advanced members collaborate on impactful technical work.

Mission

To cultivate curiosity, innovation, and practical engineering skills by encouraging students to explore, build, and experiment with robotics and emerging

Vision

To develop a vibrant technical ecosystem at NIT Patna where students contribute meaningfully to advancements in robotics, automation, and interdisciplinary technologies.

Club Activities & Focus Areas

  • Competitions: Participation in inter-college robotics competitions, hackathons, and technical challenges
  • Workshops: Regular sessions on Arduino, sensors, embedded systems, programming, and robotics tools
  • Projects: Student-driven projects in robotics, IoT, AI-based systems, and automation
  • Research & Innovation: Encouraging idea-based projects, prototypes, and technical exploration
  • Mentorship: Seniors guiding juniors through structured learning and collaboration
